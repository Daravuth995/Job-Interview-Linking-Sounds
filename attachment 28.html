Comprehension Questions

1. What is Lina's degree in?

Business Management

2. Which two areas of marketing is she interested in?

Digital marketing and brand strategy

3. What does Lina admire about the company?

Innovative campaigns and prioritizing customer engagement

4. What are her three main strengths?

Clear communication, adaptability, and time-management skills

5. What is one weakness she mentions?

Being meticulous, which sometimes slows her down

6. How did Lina solve the problem in her group project?

She delegated tasks effectively, helping the group finish ahead of schedule

7. What question does she ask the interviewer?

About the onboarding process and opportunities for professional development

8. What does the company provide for new employees?

A two-week onboarding program and regular training sessions
